U_CAPI UTextOffset ubrk_preceding (UBreakIterator *bi, UTextOffset offset)

Determine the text boundary preceding the specified offset

Documentation

Determine the text boundary preceding the specified offset. The value returned is always smaller than offset, or UBRK_DONE.
Returns:
The text boundary preceding offset, or UBRK_DONE.
Parameters:
bi - The break iterator to use.
offset - The offset to begin scanning.
