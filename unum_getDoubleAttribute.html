CAPI double unum_getDoubleAttribute (const UNumberFormat* fmt, UNumberFormatAttribute attr)

Get a numeric attribute associated with a UNumberFormat

Documentation

Get a numeric attribute associated with a UNumberFormat. An example of a numeric attribute is the number of integer digits a formatter will produce.
Returns:
The value of attr.
Parameters:
fmt - The formatter to query.
attr - The attribute to query; e.g. UNUM_ROUNDING_INCREMENT.
