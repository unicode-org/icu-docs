International Components for Unicode

ICU Developer's Workshop Program

 
Pre-requisite:
  • Understanding of C/C++ programming languages.
  • Your own laptop with one of the listed reference development environment installed. Please see the list at the bottom of this URL http://oss.software.ibm.com/icu/userguide/ICUDesign.html.
  • Successfully built the ICU 1.6 release with all the tests (intltest and cintltst) running on your laptop.
  • Fully understand at least two of the ICU samples in $Work/icu/source/samples directory, e.g. udate and ucal.
  • Downloaded, compiled and ran the workshop exercise modules from http://oss.software.ibm.com/icu/workshop.
  • Read the top-level sections of the ICU user's guide at http://oss.software.ibm.com/icu/userguide/. (Reading the full user's guide is recommended.)
