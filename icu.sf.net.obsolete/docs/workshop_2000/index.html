International Components for Unicode

Developer's Workshop

The ICU developer's workshop is to educate and share information regarding the ICU development efforts. A variety of workshop sessions cover current topics related to Unicode, software internationalization, present and the future development direction of ICU. This workshop is an ideal place to interact and exchange experiences with other fellow ICU enthusiasts. Participants receive copies of workshop proceedings. Proceedings from the workshop will be video taped as well. The First workshop is organized and sponsored by IBM and RealNames.

The workshop registration is FREE!! Seating is limited on a first-come-first-serve basis.
