Useful constant for days of week
Useful constant for days of week. Note: Calendar day-of-week is 1-based. Clients who create locale resources for the field of first-day-of-week should be aware of this. For instance, in US locale, first-day-of-week is set to 1, i.e., UCAL_SUNDAY.
