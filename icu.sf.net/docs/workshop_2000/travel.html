Public Transportation
There is a free shuttle on weekday mornings and afternoons from the Sunnyvale Caltrain station to offices on DeAnza Blvd. Apple and Sun sponsor the shuttle, but as far as we know anyone can ride it (No one has ever been "carded" - just get on the shuttle and act like you belong). The tables below give the times the shuttle arrives at / departs from the stop on Lazaneo, across the street from IBM building. The shuttle is very convenient - it leaves soon after trains arrive in the morning and gets to the train station a few minutes before trains arrive.
Caltrain has complete train schedules online.
